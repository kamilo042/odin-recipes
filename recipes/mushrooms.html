Mushroom Soup

Description

This homemade creamy mushroom soup is a comforting and satisfying dish, perfect for chilly evenings or when you're in need of some cozy comfort food. Made with fresh mushrooms, aromatic herbs, and rich cream, this soup is bursting with flavor and sure to warm you up from the inside out. It's easy to make and pairs wonderfully with a crusty slice of bread or a simple green salad. Whether you're a vegetarian looking for a hearty meal or just a fan of delicious soups, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown, about 8-10 minutes.
  4. Stir in minced garlic, thyme, and rosemary, and cook for an additional 2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Sprinkle flour over the mushroom mixture and stir to combine, cooking for another 2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.
  6. Gradually pour in vegetable broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  7. Bring the soup to a simmer and let it cook for 15-20 minutes until the flavors meld together and the soup thickens slightly.
  8. Stir in heavy cream and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Remove from heat and serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

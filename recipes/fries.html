French Fries

Description

These homemade French fries are crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside, making them the perfect side dish or snack for any occasion. Whether you're serving them alongside a burger or enjoying them on their own, these fries are sure to satisfy your cravings for a classic comfort food. With just a few simple ingredients and easy-to-follow steps, you can whip up a batch of delicious French fries in no time.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into even-sized sticks, about 1/4 inch thick.
  2. Rinse the potato sticks under cold water to remove excess starch.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 325째F (160째C).
  4. Working in batches, carefully add the potato sticks to the hot oil and fry for about 5 minutes, until they are just starting to soften but have not yet browned.
  5. Remove the partially cooked fries from the oil and let them drain on paper towels.
  6. Increase the temperature of the oil to 375째F (190째C).
  7. Return the partially cooked fries to the hot oil and fry for an additional 2-3 minutes, until they are golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove the fries from the oil and drain them on fresh paper towels.
  9. Season the fries with salt while they are still hot.
  10. Serve immediately and enjoy!
